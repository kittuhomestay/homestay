About Us

Welcome to Kittu Homestay, your peaceful retreat nestled near the scenic town of Chandragiri, just opposite the popular Bluland Water Park. We’re a family-run homestay offering a warm, personalized experience for guests looking to explore the rich culture, heritage, and natural beauty of the region.

At Kittu Homestay, we believe in creating a space where you feel at home-away from home. Our goal is simple: to offer you a clean, safe, and comfortable stay with all the essentials that make travel enjoyable.

What Makes Us Special:

  • 🛏️ Spacious & Clean Rooms: Thoughtfully designed rooms equipped with modern amenities.
  • 🌐 Free High-Speed Wi-Fi: Stay connected throughout your stay.
  • 🌳 Peaceful Location: Located in a calm neighborhood, ideal for families and solo travelers alike.
  • 🅿️ Ample Parking Space: Hassle-free parking for your convenience.
  • 🎥 24x7 CCTV Surveillance: For enhanced safety and peace of mind.
  • 🙏 Personalized Hospitality: We’re here to assist you with local tips, transport, and anything you need during your stay.

Whether you’re here for a short getaway or an extended vacation, Kittu Homestay is designed to meet the needs of every guest-from families and working professionals to solo explorers and couples.

We look forward to hosting you and making your stay truly memorable!
